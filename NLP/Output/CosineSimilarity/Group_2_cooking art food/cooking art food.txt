Cooking is the art of preparing food by combining ingredients and using various techniques such as baking, grilling, and frying. It is both a practical and creative skill, allowing individuals to explore cultural traditions and experiment with flavors. From simple home-cooked meals to elaborate gourmet dishes, the culinary world is diverse and ever-evolving. Techniques, tools, and the way food is presented have transformed cooking into a respected form of art that influences culture and lifestyle.
