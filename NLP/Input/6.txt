Mental health is an essential component of overall well-being. It includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being, affecting how people think, feel, and act. Mental health disorders, such as depression, anxiety, and schizophrenia, are common but often overlooked in many healthcare systems. There is a growing recognition of the importance of mental health, leading to greater awareness and treatment options. Healthcare professionals are working toward integrating mental health care with physical health care to improve overall patient outcomes.
